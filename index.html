ME IN MY WORK

A structure-oriented local designer who seeks options between oriental and western styles.


I grew up on an island in Hong Kong which is where the story starts. Coming along in a traditional atmosphere is how I get influenced. The very first time I had fast food was at a cantonese cafe, I can never forget the taste and the vibe. I became a huge fan of local culture and always use it as inspiration.
